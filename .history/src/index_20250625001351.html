Maldives

Ari Atoll, also called Alif or Alifu Atoll, is one of the largest natural atolls in the Maldives, located just 30 minutes by seaplane from Malé. The south is a protected marine area known for year-round whale shark sightings, while the north is famous for rare hammerhead sharks seen during deep dives.
